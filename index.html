Bank Defender

Reclaiming Regional Capital. Defending Community Banks Nationwide.

The National Challenge

Interchange leakage is a systemic threat to the community banking model. Every year, billions in revenue flow from regional institutions to out-of-state giants. Bank Defender is launching to provide the Agentic AI Sidecar infrastructure needed to identify this flight in real-time and help banks provide their members with a compelling reason to keep their capital home.

Market Agnostic

While we are starting in Nashville, our AI stack is architected to scale across any geographic landscape—securing local economies nationwide.

Revenue Defense

We quantify the exact dollar amount of leakage, providing a data-backed roadmap for institutional capital recovery.

Community Impact

By capturing lost revenue, regional banks can fund the local creators and small businesses that drive the "American Dream."

Founding Vision: Matt Thompson

Matt Thompson’s journey in finance began as a Bank Teller for Citizens Bank in East Tennessee. He saw firsthand that a community’s strength is tied to the health of its local bank. Today, Matt is bringing 7 years of Cloud Technology experience—including deep roots in the Google Cloud ecosystem—to solve the "Big Leak" for regional banks.


Based in Nashville, Matt is looking for visionary banking partners to join the Bank Defender pilot.
