The Mission

Bank Defender was built to solve the "Nashville Leakage." Every year, millions in interchange revenue flow out of local banks to out-of-state giants. We provide community banks with the Agentic AI Sidecar tools needed to identify this leakage and redirect capital back into the local economy. We help you invest in your community by keeping every "swipe" where it belongs: home.

Meet the Founder: Matt Thompson

Raised in Morristown, TN, Matt learned early on that a community is only as strong as its local support systems. His career in finance began at the ground floor, working as a Bank Teller for Citizens Bank in Johnson City while in college. He saw firsthand the vital role local banks play in the lives of their customers.


Since 2019, Matt has called Nashville home, bringing 7 years of Cloud Technology experience to the table. Having managed enterprise architecture within the Google Cloud ecosystem, Matt currently drives strategy for a Google Cloud Premier Partner. He bridges the gap between breaking-edge tech and community values—helping regional banks scale with the same "Big Tech" logic used by the world's largest financial institutions.
